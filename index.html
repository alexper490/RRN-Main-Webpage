A New Era of Chemistry Research.

Brought to you by the Gilmore Lab, the Remote Research Network ushers in a new age of scientific innovation and collaboration. Run chemical experiments and generate real data using open automated instrumentation. Share data, procedures, and analyses in an open, collaborative environment.

  • Sign Up

How does it work?

  • Get signed up

    Request to make an account via our user-friendly sign-in page. We value your interest and assure you that our team will diligently review your request within a span of fewer than seven days, ensuring a swift response.

  • Start a project

    Create procedures for on- and offline experimentation, generate and upload raw/processed analytical results, organize and categorize results, and automatically generate supporting information documentation.

  • Run our machines from anywhere

    Break free from the confines of a physical lab, using a decentralized network of automated instrumentation. Oversee and execute your reactions in real-time, from anywhere, with no previous experience required.

Why should you use it?

Eliminate geographical barriers. Connect with chemists and researchers globally. Share insights, work together on projects and experiments, and accelerate the rate of scientific exploration.
